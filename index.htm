    how it works

    Three simple steps to start earning while saving the planet. No complicated setup, just scan and go.

    01

    Scan

    Open the app and scan the QR code on any EcoCashPoint smart bin to start your recycling session.

    02

    Throw

    Deposit your recyclable items. Our AI automatically identifies and sorts materials for proper recycling.

    03

    Earn

    Get instant cash-back rewards credited to your wallet. Withdraw anytime or use for eco-friendly purchases.
